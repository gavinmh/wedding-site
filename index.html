Accomodations & Airport

We're so excited to see you at the wedding! There are lots of hotels in the surrounding towns of Cornelius, Huntersville and Mooresville but we recommend staying in Davidson at the Homewood Suites. Ask for the Ringle-Hackeling wedding to receive the group rate for June 10th and June 11th. If you would prefer to stay with local friends and family please contact either Hallie or Gavin.

The closest airport to Davidson is the Charlotte-Douglas International Airport (CLT). The airport is about half an hour from Davidson and has many rental car options. Uber is also available in Charlotte.
